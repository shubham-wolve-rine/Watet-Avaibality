About Us

The WRF's mission is to advance the science of water by funding research, providing education and training, and promoting collaboration among stakeholders in the water industry.

WHAT WE DO

One of the primary functions of the WRF is to fund research related to water. The organization provides grants to researchers and institutions around the world to support projects that advance our understanding of water quality, treatment, and distribution.

WHAT WE CAN DO

Educatioin and Training

The organization offers a variety of courses, workshops, and webinars on topics such as water treatment, distribution, and management stay up-to-date on the latest developments in the field.

Partnerships

The WRF's partners include water utilities, government agencies, academic institutions, and private companies. By bringing together diverse perspectives and expertise, the organization is able to drive innovation and advance the science of water.

Future Directions

The WRF will focus on areas such as water reuse, emerging contaminants, and innovative treatment technologies. By staying at the forefront of water research, the organization aims to ensure a sustainable and secure water future for all.
